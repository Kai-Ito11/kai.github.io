Tips about Coffee

Stages of roasting

Even with the same green beans, changing the degree of roasting produces coffee with different flavors. Lightly roasted beans have a strong acidity, and the darker the roast, the stronger the bitterness.

Grind Size & Extraction Method

Grinding is the process of grinding roasted coffee into fine particles. It is a process to make uniform particles, increase the surface area that comes in contact with hot water, and make it easy to extract ingredients. The grinding method depends on the equipment that brews the coffee.
